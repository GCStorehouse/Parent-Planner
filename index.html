52 chapters

A year of guidance

The planner walks you through your entire first year, week by week. Early chapters cover the newborn essentials - sleeping, feeding, startle reflex, swaddling, registering your baby, and those first health visitor appointments. As the weeks go on it moves through teething, vaccinations, getting out and about, socialising, and thinking about childcare.

The middle section covers weaning in depth - when to start, what to buy, how to batch cook, and recipes to try. Later chapters tackle bigger milestones: baby-proofing, day trips, restaurants, travelling abroad, and planning that all-important first birthday.

Every chapter arrives at the right moment - so the advice about weaning lands just as you're starting to think about it, and the travel guide appears when a holiday feels like a real possibility again.
